College Health Care Guide

Physical Health

Regular Health Check-ups :

Visit campus health services for routine exams or minor illnesses.
Stay updated on vaccines, such as flu shots.

Healthy Eating :

Choose balanced meals in dining halls (include fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains).
Limit processed snacks and sugary drinks.
Budget-Friendly Tips: Learn simple cooking skills and plan meals.

Exercise

Stay active with at least 30 minutes of exercise 5 days a week.
Take advantage of campus gyms, fitness classes, or intramural sports.

Mental Health

Stress Management

Practice mindfulness techniques like meditation or yoga.
Take breaks during study sessions and avoid burnout.

Time Management

Use planners or apps to organize classes, assignments, and personal time.
Avoid overcommitting to extracurricular activities.

Academic and Physical Environment

Ergonomics

Use proper posture while studying or working on a computer.,br> Take regular breaks to prevent eye strain and body fatigue.

Clean Living Space

Keep your dorm or apartment clean to prevent illnesses.
Manage allergens, pests, and proper ventilation.
